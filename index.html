About Me

After finishing my undergrad at California Lutheran University, I am currently seeking my M.S. in Agricultural and Resource Economics at Colorado State University. Experience in applied economic analysis, data science, and information systems and a seasoned user in R, SQL, and Python. Solving complex problems is done through effective analysis, based on empiricle evidence with a focus on equitable outcomes. Whether it's been troubleshooting IT problems, conducting policy analysis, or driving a tractor, taking the extra steps to do things the right way the first time has always been a priorty to me.

In high school I competed in Cross Country and Track & Field, but I've since ventured into ultimate frisbee and mountain biking to stay active. Other hobbies I really enjoy are PC gaming, collecting vinyl records, and baking for my partner, Lindsey. She claims she's loved everything I've made, but I think that's because she's just being nice. As for my records, I mainly collect folk rock, country western, and dream pop.
